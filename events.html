4Rosie

A number of Rosie's friends have very kindly taken on challenges and raced in Rosie's name and raised money for charities. Our deepest thanks and heartfelt congratulations on completing these challenges. A lot of sweat and tears.

Millie Wilson
Manchester & Leeds Marathons, 50km Ultra & Yorkshire Three Peaks

Taking on a series of endurance challenges in memory of Rosie and their dear friend Alfie, raising money equally for MIND and CRY (Cardiac Risk in the Young). Running has been a source of focus and purpose through some of their hardest moments — completing these challenges together is their truest way of honouring two young people who meant the world to them.
